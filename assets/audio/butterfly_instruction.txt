Butterfly Origami Audio Instructions

Step 1: Start with a square piece of paper, colored side up.
Step 2: Fold the paper in half diagonally to create a triangle.
Step 3: Fold the top corners down to meet at the center point.
Step 4: Fold the bottom point up to create the butterfly body.
Step 5: Fold the wings by creating diagonal creases.
Step 6: Open the wings to form the butterfly shape.
